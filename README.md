This GitHub file provides R Script for statistical analysis used in a study at the University of Cambridge, Department of Physiology, Development, and Neuroscience. Prior to data collection, favourable ethical opinion was obtained from the Human Biology Research Comittee. 
Cecilia Brassett acted as principle investigator and supervisor, Thomas Van Parys conceptualised the study and performed data collection. 
Study participants were anonymised volunteers who were staff or students within the Human Anatomy Centre, University of Cambridge.
